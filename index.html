这不是空模板，而是用你本项目的真实内容写出来的完整一页（NIH Specific Aims 标准结构：开场钩子 → 知识缺口 → 长期目标+假设 → 三个 Aim → 收尾影响）。左看骨架的作用，右看已填好的真实句子。换个课题时，把方括号 […] 换成你的即可。

段落 · 作用✅ 真实范例（AMPK–TDMD 旗舰项目）
① 开场钩子
1 句点出重要生物学问题		MicroRNAs are degraded on a physiological timescale by target-directed miRNA decay (TDMD), in which the ZSWIM8 ubiquitin ligase recognizes an AGO2–miRNA complex engaged by a highly complementary target and marks AGO2 for destruction.
② 知识缺口
已知什么 / 缺什么		Although the ZSWIM8 TDMD machinery is now structurally defined, how it is tuned by cellular metabolic state is unknown. Whether the energy sensor AMPK—activated under nutrient stress—directly controls the rate of miRNA turnover has not been tested.
③ 长期目标 + 中心假设
你想解决什么 + 一句可证伪假设		Our long-term goal is to define metabolic control of miRNA stability. Our central hypothesis is that AMPK phosphorylates ZSWIM8 at S608, accelerating TDMD of anti-fibrotic miR-29 and thereby encoding a metabolic memory of prior nutrient stress. This hypothesis is based on our motif scan placing S608 in a textbook AMPK recognition context (…KRR-S608…) within a disordered, kinase-accessible region.
④ Aim 1
建立分子事件（最可控）		Aim 1. Test whether AMPK directly phosphorylates ZSWIM8 at S608. In vitro kinase assays with recombinant AMPK + ZSWIM8 fragments, phospho-specific detection, and S608A/S608D mutants will establish direct phosphorylation and its dependence on the predicted motif.
⑤ Aim 2
连到功能读出		Aim 2. Determine how S608 phosphorylation controls the rate of TDMD. Using miR-29 decay kinetics in ZSWIM8-reconstituted cells (S608A / S608D / WT), we will measure whether phospho-mimetic S608D accelerates, and S608A blunts, miRNA turnover under AMPK activation.
⑥ Aim 3
拔高到疾病/记忆		Aim 3. Test whether AMPK-driven ZSWIM8 activity encodes a metabolic memory in a fibrosis model. In intestinal/cardiac fibrosis organoids, we will ask whether a transient nutrient-stress pulse produces a durable, S608-dependent drop in miR-29 and a lasting pro-fibrotic shift.
⑦ 收尾影响
成功 = 领域获得什么		Successful completion will establish the first metabolic input to the TDMD machinery, define a phospho-switch (S608) as a druggable node, and open metabolic control of miRNA stability as a new axis in fibrosis and metabolic memory.

🧱 完整六部分骨架 — 把一页 Aims 扩成整份提案（真实 ZSWIM8-6 内容）

点开每节看已填好的真实段落。上方 Aims 是核心；这里补齐 Significance / Innovation / Approach / Career，凑成 K99/R01 全长。

1.2 Significance（意义 · R01/K99 全长，fellowship 压缩）
  • 临床负担：代谢记忆使糖尿病并发症在血糖控制后仍持续，无疗法可擦除；代谢层面调控 miRNA 降解是机制全新的切入点。
  • 机制推进：确立 AMPK 为 TDMD 上游调控者，把 miRNA 周转从"看家过程"变为"信号响应、可成药"通路。
  • 概念广度：ZSWIM8 管控众多 miRNA 的降解，代谢输入影响全 miRNAome，而非单条。
  • 独家资源：T2D 猪模型 + 肠道/心脏类器官 + 实验室原生小 RNA 测序 + 碱基/先导编辑，可在人类相关的大动物场景检验，他人难复制。
1.3 Innovation（创新 · R01/K99 用）
  • 概念：范式转变——miRNA 降解非恒定，而由代谢状态经"激酶→连接酶"轴设定。
  • 新靶点：ZSWIM8-S608 是（据我们所知）首个把能量感应耦合到 TDMD 的磷酸化位点。
  • 方法：透明 AMPK 底物打分器 + 内源磷酸模拟编辑 + 单 miRNA 衰减动力学，此前未用于 TDMD。
  • 编辑：碱基/先导编辑装入内源 S608A/S608D 等位，避免过表达假象。
1.4 Approach（方案 · 每 Aim = 假设→设计→读出→预期→备选→陷阱）

Aim 1. 依据：打分器把 S608（…KRR-S608…）标在无序区。设计：重组 AMPK + ZSWIM8 片段激酶反应 ±AMP、磷酸特异检测、S608A/S608D。预期：AMP 依赖、S608A 消除。备选：不足则查邻近 S609/S1202（打分器也标出）+ 筛 AMPK 无关激酶。陷阱：S608D 未必完全模拟磷酸化 → 用体外磷酸化蛋白佐证。

Aim 2. 设计：ZSWIM8-KO 细胞回补 WT/S608A/S608D，转录关闭后时序小 RNA 测序。读出：每条 miRNA 衰减常数 k。预期：S608D>WT>S608A>KO。统计：80% 效力检出 30% k 差异（α=0.05），≥3 生物学重复。陷阱：全局 vs 选择性 → 对 TDMD 不敏感 miRNA 归一。

Aim 3. 设计：(i) T2D 猪组织受控代谢挑战；(ii) 类器官给定营养应激脉冲，追踪 7–14 天；先导编辑装内源 S608A/S608D。读出：应激撤除后 miRNA 与表型的持久变化。预期：瞬时应激 → 持久、S608 依赖的抑制（代谢记忆）。备选：类器官持久性不足则延长/重复脉冲。

严谨性：两性纳入、性别作为生物学变量；抗体/细胞系/构建体按 RRID 认证；go/no-go 里程碑——Aim 1 须先确认 S608 磷酸化，Aim 2 才启动。

1.5 Candidate & Career（候选人与职业发展 · 仅 K99/R00·fellowship·优青/NRF 需要）
  • 过渡逻辑（K99 导师期）：获得定量 RNA 衰减测量 + 大动物代谢建模的进阶训练，带入独立项目。
  • 独立性（R00 期）：建立"miRNA 稳定性代谢调控"的独立项目，与导师的 TDMD 结构方向划清界限。
  • 代表作：7 篇一作（RNA 生物学 + 代谢疾病）证明领导跨学科项目的能力。
  • 导师团队：覆盖 TDMD 生化、激酶信号、类器官疾病建模——提案每种方法学都有人罩。

③ 成功范例去哪找(Sample Applications)

学写作最有效的方法就是读获批全文 + 评审意见摘要。NIH 多个研究所公开分享:

  • NIH 中央库 — "Sample Applications & More" 汇总多所链接。
  • NIAID(过敏与传染病所)— 最全的范例集合,含 R01/R21/K/F 各系列,R01 范例尤为经典。
  • NIDCD — 提供 R01 等范例。
  • NIA(老龄化所)— 有 K99/R00 范例,含首次失败 vs 再次成功的完整对比,极具学习价值。
  • NIH RePORTER — 用你领域关键词搜已资助项目,读摘要学选题与措辞;可尝试礼貌联系 PI 索取申请书。
  • 关键词搜索 — 直接搜 "NIH K99 sample application"、"NIH R01 sample [你的领域]"。

⚠️ 正确用法:范例是学结构、逻辑、讲故事方式的"模板",不是照抄内容的对象。抄袭会触发查重与学术不端。

▸ 展开:NIH RePORTER 实操检索式(找你领域的已资助 K/R 项目)
  1. 打开 reporter.nih.gov → Advanced Search。
  2. Text Search 框填(和 Pivot 一样用宽 RNA 词,别用 ZSWIM8/TDMD):
    microRNA OR "non-coding RNA" OR "RNA decay" ,Search in = Projects / Terms + Abstract。
  3. Funding Mechanism 勾 K99/R00 或 R01;Activity Code 填 K99/R00/R01。
  4. Fiscal Year 选近 3 年,看最新中标选题与措辞。
  5. 点进项目 → 读 Abstract + Public Health Relevance(这两段就是浓缩版 Significance),记 PI 姓名 → 去其机构主页/邮件礼貌索取完整申请书。

RePORTER 只给摘要,不给全文;全文范例还得靠 NIAID/NIA 的 Sample Applications 页,或 PI 本人分享。

④ 逐节写作模板(以 NIH K99/R00 / R01 的 Research Strategy 为核心)

通用骨架:几乎所有 research proposal 都是这个顺序

  1. Specific Aims / 研究目标(最重要,先写) — 1 段背景引入 → 关键知识缺口 → 中心假说 → 2–3 个 Aim(每个一句话目标)→ 1 段"预期学到什么、为何重要"。
  2. Significance / 意义 → Innovation / 创新 → Approach / 方案(篇幅主体) → Timeline + Rigor。

你的 AMPK–TDMD 提案已是这个结构 → 改投任何基金,本质是"重排 + 套壳 + 调篇幅",不是从零写。

Overseas Outstanding Young Scholars / RGC ECS / NRF Fellowship all require you to first establish an appointment relationship with the host institution (signed letter of intent, or already an assistant professor). ③ Where to Find Successful Examples (Sample Applications)

The most effective way to learn grant writing is to read full funded applications + summary reviewer comments. Several NIH institutes publicly share these:

  • NIH Central Repository — "Sample Applications & More" aggregates links from multiple institutes.
  • NIAID (National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases) — the most comprehensive collection of examples, covering R01/R21/K/F series, with the R01 examples being especially classic.
  • NIDCD — provides R01 and other examples.
  • NIA (National Institute on Aging) — has K99/R00 examples, including a complete comparison of an initial unsuccessful attempt vs. a later successful one, which is extremely instructive.
  • NIH RePORTER — search funded projects using keywords from your field, read abstracts to learn topic selection and phrasing; you can try politely contacting the PI for their application.
  • Keyword search — directly search "NIH K99 sample application", "NIH R01 sample [your field]".

⚠️ Correct usage: examples are "templates" for learning structure, logic, and storytelling, not objects to copy content from. Plagiarism will trigger similarity checks and academic misconduct flags.

▸ Expand: Practical NIH RePORTER search strategy (find funded K/R projects in your field)
  1. Open reporter.nih.gov → Advanced Search.
  2. In the Text Search box (as with Pivot, use broad RNA terms, not ZSWIM8/TDMD):
    microRNA OR "non-coding RNA" OR "RNA decay" , with Search in = Projects / Terms + Abstract.
  3. Check Funding Mechanism for K99/R00 or R01; fill in Activity Code as K99/R00/R01.
  4. Select the last 3 years for Fiscal Year to see the latest funded topics and phrasing.
  5. Click into a project → read the Abstract + Public Health Relevance (these two sections are a condensed version of Significance), note the PI's name → politely reach out via their institutional homepage/email to request the full application.

RePORTER only provides abstracts, not full text; for full-text examples you'll still need the NIAID/NIA Sample Applications pages, or the PI sharing directly.

④ Section-by-Section Writing Templates (centered on the NIH K99/R00 / R01 Research Strategy)

General skeleton: almost every research proposal follows this order

  1. Specific Aims / Research Objectives (most important, write first) — 1 paragraph of background introduction → key knowledge gap → central hypothesis → 2–3 Aims (one-sentence goal each) → 1 closing paragraph on "what's expected to be learned and why it matters".
  2. Significance → Innovation → Approach (main body) → Timeline + Rigor.

Your AMPK–TDMD proposal already follows this structure → adapting it for any grant is essentially "reordering + reformatting + adjusting length," not writing from scratch.
